About Us

Who We Are

Wicks and Pages celebrates every moments, places, characters, stories, books and movies we all loved. With every candle we aim to make the story of the book you are reading come alive or bring back the memory of a book long forgotten. Each of our candle is hand-poured in small batches, here in Dubai with 100% soy wax, lead-free & zinc-free cotton wicks. Phthalate free, carcinogen free and non toxic fragrance oils infused with essential oils.

Our Story

Wicks and Pages was born after a love of two sisters for the cozy feeling that burning candles brings while reading our favorite book. Taking us to places and adventures we have never been.
